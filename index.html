Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions? We’ve Got Answers

Everything you need to know about SmartShiksha, all in one place.

Q: What is SmartShiksha?
A: SmartShiksha is a school communication and learning platform designed to connect parents, teachers, and students in Sri Lankan schools for better collaboration and academic outcomes.
Q: Who can use SmartShiksha?
A: Parents, teachers, and students in Grades 6 to 11 can use SmartShiksha. Schools and administrators also benefit from streamlined communication and reporting tools.
Q: Is SmartShiksha free?
A: Yes, SmartShiksha is free. Schools can choose the best option based on their needs and scale.
Q: Does SmartShiksha support Sinhala and English?
A: Yes! SmartShiksha has bilingual support with real-time Sinhala-English translation, making it accessible for everyone.
Q: Can schools track student progress?
A: Yes, SmartShiksha provides tools for teachers and parents to monitor student attendance, grades, and performance reports.
Q: How can I contact support?
A: You can reach SmartShiksha support via the “Contact Us” section in the website.
